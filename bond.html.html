FAQ

Questions, answered

Do you guarantee my bond back?+

Yes. Our bond cleans follow the agent's end-of-lease checklist, and if your property manager flags anything within 72 hours, we return and re-clean those areas free of charge.

How much does a bond clean cost?+

Pricing is fixed and based on property size and condition. Submit the quote form and you'll get an upfront price within the hour — no surprises on the day.

Are your cleaners insured and checked?+

Every cleaner is fully insured and police-checked, and trained to the real-estate inspection standard before they attend a single job.

Can you clean on the weekend or same-day?+

Absolutely. We offer same-day, next-day and weekend slots across Greater Sydney, subject to availability in your suburb.

Do I need to be home?+

No. Many clients arrange key collection or lockbox access. Just let us know when you book and we'll handle the rest.
